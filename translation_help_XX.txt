This application helps the expert in defusing bombs 
in the "Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes" game.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*FEATURES*
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Allows you to simply solve the 12 modules of the game
- Ability to handle up to 3 bombs at the same time
- Multilangual support (English, French, Hebrew)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS*
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ENTER]  -  Validate
[ESCAPE]  -  Reset the module
[DELETE]  -  Reset or start again the step (in concerned modules)
[BACKSPACE]  -  Cancel the last selection (in concerned modules)

NUMBERS  -  For "Memory" and "Morse Code" modules
LETTERS  -  For "Who's on first" and "Password" modules
